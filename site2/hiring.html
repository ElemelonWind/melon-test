Hiring Information

I'm open for hiring for website design, tutoring, Minecraft plugins, and discord bot commissions! I'm also glad to take collabs for any projects you'd like to work on with me. In addition, if you just want to talk with a fellow cs nerd, about books, music, video games, or literally anything, feel free to contact me! I love making new friends :)
