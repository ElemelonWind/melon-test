General Info

My name is Cindy, and I'm a teenager who resides in the DMV area. I'm an INFJ-T personality type (introvert, intuitive, feeling, judging, and turbulent). I'm also a Ravenpuff (Ravenclaw/Hufflepuff mix) and an Erudite/Amity divergent :)

I live with my parents and younger brother, who is four years younger than me. Whenever I'm stressed, I enjoy hanging out with him to relax. We watch TV shows (primarily Survivor) and talk about video games.

Hobbies

As you can probably tell from above, I love reading, especially dystopian and romance novels. I also enjoy musical theater, singing, and playing piano. I've participated in dozens of competitions and won numerous awards, including having the honor to perform at Carnegie Hall, New York in 2021.

Obviously, I also love coding. I started coding with Scratch around 2015, but in 2019 I was required to take a Computer Science elective at my selective high school, and I fell completely in love. My primary coding languages are Java, Javascript, and Python.

Lastly, I love playing video games, which is what sparked a lot of the inspiration for the projects I've created. I aspire to code masterpieces like Minecraft, Among Us, and any Supercell game. I play games with my friends to socialize and destress after a long day.
